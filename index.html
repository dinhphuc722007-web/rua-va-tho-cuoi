Rùa và Thỏ

"I am the fastest!"

Tired of being mocked, the Tortoise decided to challenge the Hare to a race.

The race began! "One... Two... Three... GO!"

The Hare shot off like the wind, leaving the Tortoise far behind.

Overconfident, the Hare lay down for a nap. Meanwhile, the Tortoise steadily crawled past...

"OH NO!"

The cheering woke the Hare with a start!

The Tortoise crossed the finish line first, thanks to his diligence and perseverance!

Bài học: In the end, diligence and perseverance will triumph over speed and negligence.
